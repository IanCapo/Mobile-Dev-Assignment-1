beLingual
Language Exchange - Live

Problem Statement

People sit in language courses for days and weeks, they study the very basics of their desired language, sometimes even get to an immediate level. But once the course ends and the newly acquired skills are not in use anymore they start to fade. In order for their knowledge to stick and grow, theyâ€™d have to talk to others in that language every day. But it is hard to find people with an adequate level.

Goal of the App

This app's goal is to connect people to make their language learning experience more fun and successful. We think that beLingual will help people become fluent in their desired language and we will know that we have succeeded when the beLinguals user community has grown to more than 10.000 beLinguals.
