Problem Statement

Traffic, especially in urban areas, has become unpredictable. Commuting times vary which makes it hard for people to estimate when to leave for work or for an appointment. They either wake up really early just to realise they could have spent another 15 minutes soundly asleep or sleep too long and in turn arrive late.

Goal of the App

This app will ensure that users wake up in the nick of time and never be late again. The app monitors traffic conditions for the users way to work, or other trips, and sets an alarm taking into consideration the time the user needs for the commute and also the time they need to get ready to leave. The user can define the destination, start point (or use geolocation), the time to get ready and frequency of the alarm (e.g. Monday-Friday start work at 9am).
