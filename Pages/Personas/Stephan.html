Who is Stephan?

'I'd love to chat with my clients without having to worry about being late to my next appointment'

Stephan from Berlin, Germany works as an insurance broker. His clients are spread all over the city and he has several client appointments everyday.

Stephan would love to take as much time with his clients without risking running late for the next appointment.

How would he use the app?

Stephan has 2 appointments today, he opens the app and finds himself on the main screen.

He clicks on the '+'-button to add an alarm. The alarm form opens.

On the current page Stephan can define a starting point and destination. For the starting point he has the option to use geolocation or to type in the address. He chooses geolocation and types the destination adress.

The next input is a digital clock, Stephan chooses 10:00am as the arrival time.

Stephan needs 5 minutes to get ready and 5 to go to the parking lot, he taps on the option that says 10min.

Stephan has the possibility to name the alarm. He names it 'MÃ¼ller', his first client today.

Now the only thing Stephan needs to do is confirm by clicking on the button at the bottom of the page that says 'Set alarm'. A modal appears, it says: "Make sure your GPS is turned on. To turn off alarm shake you phone or swipe to the left."

Stephan repeats steps 2 to 7 for his second appointment.

Stephan is at the office, his alarm rings at 9:35am, the message says: 'Light traffic, estimated commute 15min.' Staphan swipes off the alarm and leaves for his appointment.

Stephan is at his clients house, his alarm rings at 11:08am. A message says: 'Heavy traffic, estimated commute 32min.'
